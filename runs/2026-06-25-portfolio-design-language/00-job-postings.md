## 1. [Cyber startup — לא-מזוהה] — Product Designer (2nd designer)
- 2nd designer in small design org, fast-moving product org. New + existing features, end-to-end (research → shipped pixels). Daily with PMs, engineers, design team.
- Users span two worlds: **executives** (clarity at a glance) + **security managers** (turn clarity into work). Permission flows, admin consoles, real-time data = central challenge.
- Hands-on, high-iteration. "You won't sit in a Figma file in isolation. You'll prototype in code, prototype with AI, talk to users, ship, do it again next week."
- Own UX+UI end-to-end; lightweight user+competitive research at startup speed; contribute to/pull from existing design system; **work directly inside codebase for small UI fixes — light HTML/CSS + small React/Tailwind, AI tooling as day-to-day pair**; prototype with AI (Claude, Figma Make, v0, Lovable); push back/defend/change mind on evidence.
- Reqs: design degree (or Psych w/ HCI); **3-4 yrs desktop SaaS (hard req, must be evident in portfolio)**; portfolio showing process+final UI+complex flows, before/afters, design-system contributions, research artifacts; **Figma mastery**; **demonstrable AI tool use — "what you've actually built or shipped with them, not just that you've heard of them"**; light frontend (HTML/CSS, React/Tailwind, AI-assisted fine); excellent English; curiosity/no-ego/fast iteration; **Git basics (branches, PRs, reading a repo)**; cybersecurity/networking/admin exposure = advantage.
